TimelyVC

See and make timely comments on internet videos.

Download at Chrome Web Store!

About Timely

Timely is a chrome extension that places the power of chronologically displayed comments at your finger tips. Enjoy your favorite online videos with the addition of a comment timeline at the top. Users can post their own comments and add to the timeline to create a robust and insightful community of video commentary!
